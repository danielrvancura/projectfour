Welcome to It Takes a Village. One of the challenges special needs children face is making connections with kids their own age in their area. This site is designed to help parents with special needs children connect with other families in similar situations. We encourage parents to make connections to share their struggles and victories to help other families out. Teachers and therapists of special needs children are also welcome to join to share their insight. Whether it's making playdates, finding recommended therapists, or parents just needing to find a babysitter, It Takes a Village is here to help.
